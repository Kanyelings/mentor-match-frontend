Calling all mentors...


So you've decided to take a huge step toward responsibility. As you freely take this chance, we hope that you build the best of relationships with your mentee, as you help them skim through any difficulties that they may have.

Fill in the following form as best as you can.

You must be a level 300(year 2) student to fill in for this role.

Mentor form

Teacher

Now, don't be alarmed. Teaching here involves helping your mentee(s) think using the best methods. Helping them study efficiently, how to manage their time, how to look up resources when they are in dire need.

Agent

A mentor should be able to stand for his mentee(s). Vouching for them in technical situations, and them removing every possible obstacle that could be in their way. After the mentee has made a convincing attempt, of course.

Role Model

Remember, that your mentee looks up to you. That is why you should first of become someone who will likely impact others. Have something you would want to see in another person.

Confidante

First and foremost, your mentee(s) sees you as a friend. They trust you, and may or may not tell you things that are geared towards academics all the time. It is your duty to counsel them if they have any issue that may be hindering their success rate.
